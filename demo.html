Moving Blur

In Progress

A Gaussian blur mask that stays completely still while text scrolls behind it — frozen in place, as if glued to the screen. It only moves when the hidden content is about to escape, at which point it snaps hard back to the center of the text. Try hovering your cursor over it.

The Vanishing

There is a sentence here that you are not supposed to read. It contains something important — an address, maybe, or a name. But the glass keeps it away from you. It doesn't move. It just sits there. Scroll past it and nothing happens. The square stays exactly where it was.

Until it has to move.

Classified Entry #0041 — Clearance: Level 7 Obsidian. Access code: RHINO-7742-VELVET. Drop point confirmed. Do not acknowledge. Do not redistribute. Signal ends.

After the Redaction

You scrolled. The text moved. The square did not — it sat frozen in place, indifferent, until the very last moment when the words were about to escape. Then it snapped. Back to the center of the text, in one hard jump. Then it froze again, as if nothing had happened.

It doesn't follow you. It doesn't drift. It just watches the edges, and when something tries to leak out it re-centers in a single, abrupt movement. Try moving your cursor over it — even that gets swallowed.

/* 1. HTML structure */

<div class="blur-target-block" id="my-target">
  <p class="blur-context top">Non-sensitive label...</p>

  <div class="blur-secret" id="my-secret">
    <p>Sensitive content here</p>
  </div>

  <p class="blur-context bottom">Non-sensitive note...</p>
  <div class="moving-blur-mask" id="my-mask"></div>
</div>

/* 2. Initialize */

new MovingBlur({
  scrollContainer: '#scroll-wrapper',
  target:          '#my-target',
  secret:          '#my-secret',
  mask:            '#my-mask',
  blurAmount:      12,     // Gaussian radius (px)
  padding:         6,      // safety margin (px)
  cursorBlur:      true,
});
ParameterDescriptionDefault
blurAmount Gaussian blur radius in pixels 12
padding Safety margin (px) — how close the text can get to the mask edge before a snap triggers 6
cursorBlur Blur the mouse cursor when it hovers over the mask true
